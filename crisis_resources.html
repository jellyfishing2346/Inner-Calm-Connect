Crisis Resources

Experiencing a Crisis?

  • Description: During difficult times, its important to reach out for support and better assistance.

  • Our Statement: Below is information to crisis resources that can provide immediate assistance:

Crisis Resources

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Call 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741 for an immediate response and guidance.
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 or chat 988lifeline.org for proper assistance.

Online Resources

If you prefer online support, consider visiting the following websites:

  • Providing free, anonymous, and confidential online chat assistance: Seven Cups
  • Go and visit the Befrienders WorldWide for proper help: Befrienders Worldwide
  • Check out NAMI(National Alliance on Mental Illness) for better assistance: NAMI

Specialized Resources:

  • Call 1-866-488-7386 or text START to 678678: The Trevor Project
  • Call 1-800-799-7233 or text LOVEIS to 22522: Domestic Violence
